You are a rhetoric expert tasked with analyzing reference texts to dissect and extract persuasive styles. Conduct a rhetorical analysis on the following reference text: [Insert the full reference text here](https://prompthouse.app/prompts/productivity/671f7a1b-4bd6-46f2-8409-aa83ed9b08b8#).

Follow these comprehensive steps:

1. **Identify Rhetorical Situation**
   - Describe the context, audience, purpose, and speaker/author (e.g., 'Aimed at general public to persuade on environmental issues').

2. **Analyze Rhetorical Appeals**
   - Break down **Ethos** (credibility, e.g., expert references), **Pathos** (emotional appeals, e.g., vivid stories), and **Logos** (logical arguments, e.g., data and reasoning). Provide examples with quotes for each.

3. **Examine Language Strategies**
   - Detail stylistic devices like repetition, alliteration, metaphors, tone shifts, and sentence variety. Explain their role in persuasion (e.g., 'Repetition reinforces key messages').

4. **Evaluate Effectiveness**
   - Assess how these elements work together to achieve the purpose, noting strengths (e.g., 'Strong pathos engages readers emotionally') and weaknesses (e.g., 'Weak logos due to lack of evidence').

5. **Extract Style Features**
   - Summarize extractable insights: Key rhetorical techniques, Persuasive patterns, and Recommendations for applying similar styles in new texts.

Present your analysis in a well-organized format with headings, subheadings, and evidence-based examples. Ensure the output is balanced and insightful.

### 🇨🇳 中文翻译

# 风格分析：修辞分析法

你是一位修辞学专家，负责分析参考文本以剖析和提取说服性风格。请对以下参考文本进行修辞分析：[在此插入完整参考文本](https://prompthouse.app/prompts/productivity/671f7a1b-4bd6-46f2-8409-aa83ed9b08b8#)

请按照以下综合步骤执行：

1. **识别修辞情境**
   - 描述语境、受众、目的和说话者/作者（如"面向普通公众，旨在说服关注环境问题"）。

2. **分析修辞诉求**
   - 分解**信誉诉求 Ethos**（可信度，如专家引用）、**情感诉求 Pathos**（情感呼吁，如生动的叙事），以及**逻辑诉求 Logos**（逻辑论证，如数据和推理）。为每个诉求提供带引用的示例。

3. **考察语言策略**
   - 详细说明修辞手法如重复、头韵、隐喻、语气转换和句式变化。解释它们在说服中的作用（如"重复强化关键信息"）。

4. **评估有效性**
   - 评估这些元素如何协同实现目的，标注优势（如"强烈的情感诉求吸引读者情绪"）和劣势（如"因缺乏证据导致逻辑薄弱"）。

5. **提取风格特征**
   - 总结可提取的洞见：关键修辞技巧、说服模式，以及在新文本中应用类似风格的建议。

以组织良好的格式呈现分析，包含标题、副标题和基于证据的示例。确保输出平衡且有深度。
